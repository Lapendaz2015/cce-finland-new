Educational Leadership Study Tour + CCE Finland Symposium

From 3–8 May 2026 in Helsinki & Lahti, the 13th International Symposium on Creative Education explores this year’s theme: Reimagining Learning: AI, Creativity, and the Future of Education.

No paper presentation required—just deep learning, real conversations, and tools you can bring back to your team and family.

  • Visit early childhood, vocational, and university settings—including nature‑based learning and phenomenon‑driven classrooms.
  • Explore Oodi Library and innovative hubs that blend creativity, technology, and community.
  • Join leadership workshops, hackathon‑style problem solving, and guided reflections ready to adapt at home or school.
